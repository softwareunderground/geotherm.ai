About geotherm.ai

What? This web app doesn't do much.

Whence? Some people hacked it together at the Geothermal Hackathon.

Examples All of the results come from analysis of the IGA bibliographic database. Here are some things this web app might do one day:

  1. Geospatial analysis of the articles based on automatic geolocation of the articles. Check out the map.
  2. Keyword search based on latent semantic analysis (LSA) of the abstracts.
  3. Analysis of the clusters of LSA keywords.
  4. Generation of random geothermal papers based on an LSTM neural network trained on the titles.
  5. Graph theoretic analysis of the co-authorship network.
